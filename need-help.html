Delivery schedule

Minbart processes and ships order 6 days a week with the exception of Fridays and UAE public bank holidays. Next day delivery is available for UAE customers only. To qualify orders must be placed before 12noon to guarantee this service.

Important delivery information

Once your order have been verified and payment has been approved you will receive an order confirmation from our customer care team. Your delivery window will arrive according to the delivery option selected:

  • United Arab Emirates: 2 – 3 Business days
  • GCC: 3 – 5 Business days
  • Worldwide International: 4 – 7 Business days
  • Estimated delivery times are to be used as a guide only and commence from date of dispatch. Minbart is not responsible for any delays caused by customs clearance and processes.
  • You will always be required to sign on receipt of your delivery. Once a signature has been provided it is no longer Minbarts’ liability.
  • For customers within the United Arab Emirates shipping is payable on delivery
  • For GCC customers shipping and customs is payable on delivery
  • For Worldwide international customers for shipping and customs is payable on delivery.
  • Our shipping partner is Aramex
  • Once your order is placed you can track your order online by entering your tracking code to www.aramex.com
  • For any other questions or enquiries please contact hello@minbart.com and our customer care team will support you with your query.
  • Please note, at the time of of checkout you will be notified if we have to ship your order in multiple shipments, which may result in multiple postings to the cardholder’s monthly statement.

How do I make a return after purchasing in-store?

Minbart promises to exchange any items that you are not completely happy with when you return it to us is a re-sellable condition in store within 10 days of date on receipt.

For hygiene reasons, we will not offer exchanges on pierced jewelry, underwear or swimwear if the hygiene seal has been removed, unless they are of unsatisfactory quality or unfit for purpose.

Please note that items must be returned with all barcodesand description tags intact. Your item(s) should be in their original packaging. Any shoes returned to Minbart store must not be worn, and must be returned in their original box.

We are unable to offer exchanges at this time.

How do I make a return after purchasing online?

Returning online items with Minbart is simple. If you are not completely happy with your purchase, you can return it within 10 days of receipt to the Minbart store.

Please ensure you have your personalized online invoice (as proof of purchase) and debit or credit card used for the transaction.

Refunds will be done only through the Original Mode of Payment.

What about my delivery charge?

Minbart will refund your delivery charge under the following conditions:

  • Minbart, by error, sent you the wrong product.
  • Minbart, by error, sent you an item that was faulty, damaged, of unsatisfactory quality, or unfit for purpose.
  • Minbart will not refund the delivery charge if there was more than one item purchased and not all is being returned.

My receipt is out of date - can I still return my item?

Minbart is happy to return items purchased online or in store within 10 days with proof of purchase. Any items that fall outside this 10 day window will not be returned unless they are of unsatisfactory quality or unfit for purpose. Minbart exercises the right to refuse refunds if we believe that the product has been used or worn and therefore no longer re-sellable.
