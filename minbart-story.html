Minbart Story

“Minbart is all about capturing, educating and investing into today’s modern creative attitude.”

Alia Al Qassimi, Minbart Founder

Minbart is truly an individual store as it markets an upbeat collection of women’s fashion – only the stylish and cool. Minbart offers a unique experience, presenting fashion-conscious customers something new outside of your everyday department stores and other boutiques.

Minbart is all about capturing, educating and investing into today’s modern creative attitude. Our goal is to always provide you with creativity that is fresh, fashion forward and authentic. Designers that explore enlighten and inspire. Brands that is conceptually interesting and visually stunning.

Minbart’s interior evokes an ambience of gentle simplicity with matte concrete flooring, stainless steel rails rested against textured white chip wood walls and a high exposed ceiling with a dotted array of suspended lights. The sleek urban and movable space design allows our designers to steal the spotlight.
